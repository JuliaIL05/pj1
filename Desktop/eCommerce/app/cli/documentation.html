Julia & Tech is a specialized eCommerce application for computer hardware and technology products. Designed specifically for sales representatives, this system streamlines the order management process for computer components, peripherals, and technology accessories.

Our platform focuses on providing an efficient workflow for technical sales teams, with features tailored to the computer hardware industry including component compatibility tracking, technical specifications management, and specialized pricing for IT products.

Purpose: Create and process customer orders for computer products

Features: - Customer information management
- Computer hardware product selection
- Quantity and pricing calculation

- Order validation and storage

5. Complete Orders View

- Customer and product details

Purpose: Locate and review individual orders

Features: - Search by unique order ID
- Detailed order information display
- Product and pricing breakdown
- Customer contact information
